Mission

Our mission is to deliver premium-quality frankincense to the global market while fostering sustainable partnerships and maintaining the highest ethical standards. We strive to create an environment of mutual support and collaboration, ensuring our products meet the most rigorous quality specifications while contributing to the economic development of our local communities.

Vision

To establish ourselves as the world's leading supplier of premium frankincense from Puntland, Somalia, while creating a sustainable and environmentally conscious supply network that empowers local communities and preserves our natural resources for future generations.
