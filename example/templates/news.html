Interview July 2024

Meet Mohamed Aden, CEO of Derman Oil

Mohamed Aden, CEO of Derman Oil, shares insights on what makes Somalia the world's premier source of frankincense and how DermanOil ensures ethical sourcing and full traceability from tree to bottle.

  • Somalia's Advantage: Unique climate, landscape, and centuries of indigenous knowledge produce the world's finest frankincense.
  • Traceability: Every batch is tracked from harvest to shipment, with rigorous quality and documentation protocols.
  • Ethical Sourcing: DermanOil partners directly with local harvesters, ensuring fair prices and sustainable practices.

Certification January 2024

Derman Oil Achieves FairWild Certification

Derman Oil has been officially certified by FairWild Foundation, reinforcing our commitment to sustainability and ethical sourcing. As a leading exporter of premium frankincense resin and essential oils from Boswellia carteri and Boswellia frereana, this certification validates our sustainable harvesting practices and community-focused approach.

Key Highlights:
  • EU Organic certified
  • Direct sourcing from Somalia
  • 10% revenue for community development
  • Full supply chain traceability
Our Mission:

Beyond premium products, we invest in preserving Boswellia trees through sustainable harvesting, education, and technical training while ensuring fair prices for local communities.
