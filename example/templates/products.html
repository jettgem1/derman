Resin Extraction Process

The resin is extracted through small, shallow incisions on the trunk or branches. As a milky substance, it coagulates upon contact with air and is carefully collected by hand. Our vast boswellia forests, existing for millennia in modern-day Somalia, produce an extremely high-quality resin.

Commiphora Myrrh Essential Oil

100% Pure, Natural, and Sustainably Sourced

Myrrh essential oil is a thick oil with a warm, earthy scent, often used for its calming and traditional healing properties. It's commonly found in perfumes as a base note, especially in rich, resin-like blends. In aromatherapy, it's used to promote relaxation and focus.

It also plays a role in pharmaceutical products like toothpaste, mouthwash, and wound care for its natural antimicrobial effects. In skincare, it's added to creams and balms for its anti-aging, soothing, and healing qualities. Myrrh oil helps reduce inflammation, fights bacteria and fungi, supports skin repair, and offers antioxidant protection.
