Our Ethical Commitment

Derman is a producer and supplier of frankincense from Africa to global markets in Asia, Middle East, Europe and USA.

We play a key role in the preservation and conservation of boswellia sacra (commonly known as frankincense or the olibanum-tree), which are predominantly found in the country of Somalia.

Our commitment to sustainability of this rare and precious tree drives every aspect of our operations, from harvesting to distribution.
